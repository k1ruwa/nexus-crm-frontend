# Nexus CRM - Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started

### What is Nexus?
Nexus is an AI-powered networking CRM that helps you capture and manage professional contacts using your camera, voice, or manual entry. It works completely offline and stores all data locally on your device.

## First Launch

When you open Nexus for the first time, you'll see an onboarding screen explaining the key features:
- 📷 **Camera Capture**: Scan business cards
- 🎤 **Voice Notes**: Record and transcribe
- ✨ **AI Enrichment**: Auto-complete profiles
- 🔔 **Reminders**: Never miss a follow-up
- 🔒 **Privacy**: All data stays local

## Adding Your First Contact

### Method 1: Camera Capture (Business Card)
1. Tap the **"+"** floating button in the bottom-right
2. Select the **Camera** option
3. Point your camera at a business card
4. Tap the **capture** button
5. Review the photo and tap **"Use Photo"**
6. Wait for AI to extract the information
7. Review and edit the auto-filled fields
8. Tap **"Add Contact"**

### Method 2: Voice Capture
1. Tap the **"+"** button
2. Select the **Voice** option
3. Tap the **microphone** button to start recording
4. Say the contact details (e.g., "I just met Sarah Johnson from Acme Corp...")
5. Tap the **square** button to stop
6. Tap **"Save"** on the recording
7. Wait for AI to transcribe and extract info
8. Review and complete the fields
9. Tap **"Add Contact"**

### Method 3: Manual Entry
1. Tap the **"+"** button
2. Select the **Manual** option
3. Fill in the contact information
4. Optionally use **"Auto-enrich"** to find social profiles
5. Add tags (comma-separated)
6. Tap **"Add Contact"**

## Viewing Contacts

### Dashboard
The main screen shows:
- **Search bar**: Find contacts by name, company, or tag
- **Stats cards**: Total contacts and upcoming reminders
- **Tabs**: Filter by All, Recent (last 7 days), or AI Enriched
- **Contact cards**: Tap any card to view full details

### Contact Details
Tap a contact card to see:
- Full profile information
- Social media links
- Notes
- Voice notes
- Reminders
- Last contacted date

## Adding Voice Notes

1. Open a contact
2. Tap **"Record"** in the Voice Notes section
3. Record your note
4. Tap **Stop** when done
5. Tap **"Save"**
6. The note will be transcribed automatically

Voice notes are perfect for:
- Meeting summaries
- Conversation highlights
- Action items
- Context for future follow-ups

## Managing Reminders

### Adding a Reminder
1. Open a contact
2. Tap **"Add"** in the Reminders section
3. Enter title (e.g., "Follow up on proposal")
4. Add description (optional)
5. Select date and time
6. Tap **"Add Reminder"**

### Viewing All Reminders
1. Tap the **bell icon** in the top-right of the Dashboard
2. View reminders by: Upcoming, Overdue, or Completed
3. Tap a reminder to open the contact
4. Check the box to mark as complete

## Organizing Contacts

### Tags
Add tags to contacts for easy organization:
- Client
- Prospect
- Conference
- VIP
- Partner

Tags appear on contact cards and are searchable.

### Search
Use the search bar to find contacts by:
- Name
- Company
- Email
- Tags

### Filtering
Use the tabs on the Dashboard:
- **All**: Show all contacts
- **Recent**: Contacts added in the last 7 days
- **AI Enriched**: Contacts with AI-enhanced data

## Installing as an App

### On Mobile (iOS/Android)
1. Open Nexus in your browser
2. Look for the **install banner** at the bottom
3. Tap **"Install"**
4. Or use browser menu → "Add to Home Screen"

### On Desktop
1. Look for the install icon in the address bar
2. Click to install
3. Or click the install banner

### Benefits of Installing
- Quick access from home screen
- Full-screen experience
- Works offline
- Faster load times

## Privacy & Data

### What's Stored Locally
- All contact information
- Voice recordings (as audio files)
- Business card photos
- Reminders
- Notes

### What's NOT Stored
- Nothing is sent to a server
- No cloud backup (local-only)
- No analytics or tracking

### Data Backup
**Important**: Your data is stored in your browser's local storage. To backup:
1. Use browser export features
2. Don't clear browser data
3. Future: Export to vCard/CSV (coming soon)

## Permissions

Nexus needs permission for:
- **Camera**: To scan business cards
- **Microphone**: To record voice notes

Permissions are requested only when needed and can be revoked anytime in browser settings.

## Tips & Tricks

### Camera Capture
- Use good lighting
- Keep card flat and in focus
- Ensure all text is visible
- Works best with standard business card layouts

### Voice Capture
- Speak clearly
- Include full name, company, and contact info
- Mention context (where you met, next steps)
- Quiet environment works best

### AI Enrichment
- Enter at least email or full name for best results
- LinkedIn profiles are automatically found when available
- Review AI suggestions before saving

### Reminders
- Set reminders right after meeting someone
- Use recurring reminders for regular check-ins
- Check the Reminders page daily

### Search
- Use partial names or companies
- Tags are fully searchable
- Email domains work too (e.g., "@acme.com")

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working
- Check browser permissions
- Ensure you're using HTTPS
- Try different browser
- Check camera privacy settings

### Voice Not Recording
- Allow microphone permission
- Check system microphone settings
- Try different browser
- Ensure microphone is not in use

### Data Not Persisting
- Don't use private/incognito mode
- Don't clear browser data
- Check storage quota
- Try different browser

### App Not Installing
- Use Chrome, Safari, or Edge
- Ensure HTTPS connection
- Try manual "Add to Home Screen"
- Clear browser cache and retry
